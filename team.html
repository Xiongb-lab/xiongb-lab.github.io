Dr. Prof. Bing Xiong(熊兵), Principal Investigator

Dr. Bing Xiong is a professor of Shanghai Institute of Materia Medica (SIMM), CAS. He earned the Ph.D degree in medicinal chemistry from Chinese Academy of Sciences (2001-2003), and did the postdoctoral training in structure biology at McGill University, Canada (2003-2005). Currently, his research focuses on the structure/fragment-based drug discovery in protein post-translational modificaitons, as well as developing phage-display technology to enpower the peptide drug discovery. Over the years, he has published more than 100 research articles in peer-review journals including Journal of Medicinal Chemistry, ChemMedChem, EJMC, Bioinformatics, BMC Bioinformatics, journal of computer information and modeling, JCC. Prof. Xiong has received a number of awards including "National Talents Plan", Shanghai "Oriental Talent" and CPA-Servier Young Investigator Awards in Medicinal Chemistry. His team has transformed several achievements to drug companies, and two drug candidates have entered into clinical trials, and one of them (Gumarontinib) has been approved in China and Japan.
